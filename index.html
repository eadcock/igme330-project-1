Ecosystem Simulator

FPS: Determines how many times to step in a second
Water Spawn Rate: Percentage chance a ground tile will turn into water
Plant Grow Cooldown: How many frames before a plant may grow again
Herbivore Breeding Cooldown: How many frames before an herbivore may breed again
Carnviore Breeding Cooldown: How many frames before a carnivore may breed again
Herbivore Breeding Food Requirement: How recently must an herbivore have eaten in order to breed
Carnviore Breeding Food Requirement: How recently must a carnivore have eaten in order to breed
Herbivore Dying Rate: How many frames an herbivore can survive without food
Carnviore Dying Rate: How many frames a carnivore can survive without food
Welcome to your little ecosystem. Watch plants, herbivores, and carnivores grow, breed, and kill. Change values on the right to alter how tiles behave! Hover over a property for more information.
Unoccupied tiles are brown, and have a chance of turning into water, blue.
Green tiles, plants, will grow if an adjacent tile is water.
Herbivores, represented by yellow, eat plants and breed with each other.
Carnivores, red, hunt herbivores and breed.
